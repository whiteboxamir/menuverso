# Barcelona Lunch Neighborhoods

Key districts for "Menú del Día" research, ordered by restaurant density.

| # | Neighborhood | Character | Density |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Eixample** (Dreta + Esquerra) | Office workers, wide variety | Very High |
| 2 | **El Born / La Ribera** | Trendy, mid-range to premium | High |
| 3 | **Gràcia** | Local, bohemian, affordable | High |
| 4 | **El Raval** | Diverse, multicultural, budget-friendly | High |
| 5 | **Barri Gòtic** | Tourist-heavy but hidden gems exist | Medium |
| 6 | **Poble Sec** | Up-and-coming foodie scene | Medium |
| 7 | **Sant Antoni** | Market area, trendy, growing | Medium |
| 8 | **Barceloneta** | Seafood-focused, tourist pricing | Medium |
| 9 | **Sarrià-Sant Gervasi** | Upscale, premium menus | Low-Medium |
| 10 | **Sants** | Residential, authentic, budget | Medium |
