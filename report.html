01 · Executive Summary

Spain has no subscription dining club — yet. While Barcelona has adjacent competitors in restaurant discovery and menú del día apps (notably Menuveo with 500+ listings, and the award-winning MenuDelDia.eu), no platform offers the 2-for-1 subscription model. The €94B Spanish foodservice market — with Europe's highest dine-out frequency — is in a pre-competitive window for subscription dining.

Barcelona offers a uniquely favorable combination for a subscription dining club launch: 32M annual tourists generating continuous rotating demand, 5.6M metro population with 50% dining out weekly, an average meal spend of €27 in the sweet spot for 2-for-1 economics, and an early-stage competitive landscape with no established subscription deals player.

Menuverso has mapped 1,178 restaurants across 18 core neighborhoods with ratings, pricing, cuisine taxonomy, contact data, and geo-coordinates — the supply-side intelligence required to launch.
