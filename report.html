01 · Executive Summary

Spain has zero subscription dining clubs. No 2-for-1 platforms. No consumer dining subscriptions. The €67B Spanish foodservice market — with Europe's highest dine-out frequency — is completely uncontested in the subscription dining category.

Barcelona offers a uniquely favorable combination for a subscription dining club launch: 32M annual tourists generating continuous rotating demand, 5.6M metro population with 50% dining out weekly, an average meal spend of €27 in the sweet spot for 2-for-1 economics, and zero competitive presence in the subscription deals category.

Menuverso has mapped 1,458 restaurants across 18 core neighborhoods with ratings, pricing, cuisine taxonomy, contact data, and geo-coordinates — the supply-side intelligence required to launch.
