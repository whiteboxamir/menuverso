🎯 The Opportunity

Spain has zero subscription dining clubs. No 2-for-1 platforms. No consumer dining subscriptions. The entire Southern European restaurant deal market is uncontested.

Barcelona is Europe's 4th most-visited city with 32M+ annual tourists and a metro population of 5.6M. The restaurant landscape includes 7,500+ establishments, with dining out deeply embedded in the culture. Yet no platform offers the subscription dining club model that has scaled rapidly in Germany, the UK, and the Netherlands.

Menuverso has built the supply-side intelligence infrastructure: 1,458 restaurants across 18 neighborhoods, profiled with ratings, pricing, contact data, cuisine taxonomy, and geo-coordinates. This is the launchpad for Spain's first restaurant deal subscription.
