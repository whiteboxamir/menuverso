Strategic Assessment

💪 Strengths

  • First-mover data advantage — 1,504 restaurants catalogued, 595 verified
  • Product depth — Interactive map, CSV export, favorites, price analytics, trilingual
  • Proven model — NeoTaste validated at €21M Series A scale
  • SEO dominance — 1,507 indexed pages with JSON-LD structured data

😟 Weaknesses

  • No app yet — mitigated by PWA-first approach
  • Tiny team — lean is an advantage pre-PMF
  • Unproven revenue — Phase 1 deliberately free
  • Data maintenance — needs community + automated scraping
  • No restaurant relationships — Phase 1 creates warm intros

🌟 Opportunities

  • Multi-city expansion — Madrid, Valencia, Seville
  • Tourist market — 12M+ annual visitors to Barcelona
  • Corporate subscriptions — employee lunch perks
  • Restaurant SaaS upsell — analytics, reservations, menu tools
  • Delivery integration — Glovo/UberEats partnerships

⚠️ Threats

  • NeoTaste enters Spain — move fast; local knowledge is a moat
  • Google adds features — Google moves slowly on local features
  • Restaurant resistance — deals are free; frame as free marketing
  • Low willingness to pay — validate with beta cohorts first
  • Data quality degradation — automated verification + user flags

Risk Assessment

Key Risks & Mitigations

CRITICAL

Users Won't Pay

NeoTaste has hundreds of thousands of paying users. Spanish consumers are price-sensitive at lunch → savings have high perceived value. Mitigate: Validate with 500-person beta. If conversion <3%, pivot to restaurant-pays model.

CRITICAL

Restaurants Won't Participate

Deals are free for restaurants. Lunch is often the slowest period. Mitigate: Start with restaurants that have empty tables. Offer 100% control over deal frequency.

CRITICAL

NeoTaste Enters Spain

Speed, local knowledge, and Spanish-language menú del día specialization create a moat. Also: being acquired by NeoTaste is a valid exit.

MODERATE

Data Freshness

Menus change daily. Mitigate: Automated scraping + user-submitted photos + restaurant self-service portal.
